About Me

Passionate researcher dedicated to advancing scientific knowledge

I am a dedicated researcher with expertise in the field of AI in Medicine and Healthcare. My work focuses on medical imaging and the implications of generative AI. I have multiple years of experience the academic and industrial world and am working with Daniel Rueckert and Jiazhen Pan.

Research Areas

Exploring the frontiers of knowledge

Cardiac MRI

Conducting research on innovative artificial intelligence techniques to enhance cardiac MRI, focusing on improving image reconstruction quality and achieving higher resolution through state-of-the-art superresolution algorithms.

  • Cardiac MRI
  • Medical Imaging
  • Superresolution

Generative AI

Applying diffusion models and generative AI to healthcare and medical imaging, with a focus on exploring the spectrum between reconstruction and generation.

  • Generative AI
  • Autoregressive Generation
  • Diffusion Models

Tokenization

Developing advanced techniques to tokenize high-dimensional medical data, enabling more effective use of generative AI models in healthcare applications.

  • Autoencoders
  • Tokenization
  • High Compression
